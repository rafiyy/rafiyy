If you feel that you have been trolled, or if your name has been misrepresented, you can private message us to delete a confession. However, we do not entertain request which does not make sense(e.g. you don't like to see the word sex and you decide to delete the confession).

We do not post any advertisement, general events or related stuff on confession so please refrain from using the form to post them.

As administrators, we are purely facilitators and we let the community decide the content of the confessions. We seek your understanding in doing so.
